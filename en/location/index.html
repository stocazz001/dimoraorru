What to see in Cabras: the top 5 attractions

SEO-focused local guide to explore Sinis while staying at B&B Dimora Orrù.

1) Is Arutas Beach

Quartz-grain beach with crystal-clear water, one of Sinis landmarks.

2) San Giovanni di Sinis

Historic seaside village with authentic atmosphere.

3) Cabras Museum

Giovanni Marongiu Civic Museum, home to the Giants of Mont'e Prama exhibits.

4) Tharros archaeological area

Major coastal archaeological site with stunning sea views.

5) Mari Ermi

Iconic Sinis beach, a great addition to a Tharros visit.
