Cookie Policy

Last updated: 14 March 2026. This page explains which technologies the site uses and how you can manage consent.

How the consent banner works

When you visit the site you can choose whether optional categories should be enabled. Your choice is saved in the browser so the site remembers your preferences. You can update that choice at any time with the “Manage consent” control in the footer.

Categories used on this site

Essential technical

Required for the website to function and to store your consent choice. This category also includes local storage of the preference itself.

Analytics

If you accept this category, the site may load Google Tag Manager and any measurement tools configured in the container to analyse traffic, performance and CTA clicks.

Embedded maps and third-party services

Some pages include embedded maps and links to third-party services. These elements may create direct requests to external providers when the page loads or when you choose to open a link.

Third-party services and links

  • Google Maps: used to display the property location directly on site pages.
  • Google Tag Manager / analytics: activated only after analytics consent.
  • Booking engine: links to book.bnbdimoraorru.it open an external domain that may use its own tracking tools and cookies.
  • WhatsApp, Google, TripAdvisor and other external links: any cookies or tracking depend on the external service you choose to open.

How long consent choices are stored

Your consent preference is stored locally in the browser until you change it or clear browser data. If local browser data are removed, the banner may appear again.

How to withdraw or update consent

You can change your preferences at any time from the footer. For more detail on personal-data processing, see the Privacy policy.
