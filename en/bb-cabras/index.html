FAQ

What are the check-in and check-out times?

Check-in is from 16:00 to 23:30 and check-out is from 01:00 to 10:00. Guests should communicate arrival time in advance.

Is breakfast included?

Breakfast is served from 08:30 to 10:30, with Italian and gluten-free options. Takeaway is not available.

What are the cancellation and prepayment conditions?

Bookings made through our official direct website are non-refundable. Our official direct website always offers the best available rate.

What should I know about children and extra beds?

Children of all ages are welcome. Cots and extra beds are not available at the property.

Which house rules should I know?

Minimum check-in age is 18; guests under 18 can check in only with a parent or legal guardian. Quiet hours are from 22:00 to 07:00, bachelor/bachelorette or similar parties are not allowed, and smoking indoors is forbidden.

Are pets allowed and is parking available?

Pets are not allowed. Public parking is available nearby.
